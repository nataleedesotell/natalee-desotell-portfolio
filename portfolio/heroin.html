Heroin in Wisconsin Project

This map is just one of many created for one of the most interesting projects I have had the pleasure to be a part of. Myself, along with Clare Trainor and Jon McHugh, set out to find what the heroin epidemic looked like in Wisconsin. Finding such granular data was an arduous process, but we eventually found data from the Wisconsin Hospital Association on opioid-related hospitalizations. We compared these data over time and to race and income.

For my online portfolio version of this map, I removed the exact percentages to maintain patient privacy.
